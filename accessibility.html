Accessibility Statement

Open access

StreamSuites aims to keep every public surface navigable, readable, and keyboard-friendly. Static layouts reduce motion and minimize script requirements.

Standards

We target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast and navigation. Native elements, such as the roadmap progress bars, are preserved to keep semantics intact.

  • Focusable controls with visible states
  • Semantic headings and lists
  • Reduced reliance on animation

Feedback

Contact us if you encounter accessibility barriers.

  • Email: access@stream.suites
  • Discord: Community support
  • Dashboard users can open a support ticket from the admin view.

Roadmap

Upcoming accessibility updates will be reflected in the public changelog, with emphasis on captions, transcripts, and keyboard fallbacks.
